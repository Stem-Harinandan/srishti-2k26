Srishti 2K26 Events

Srishti 2K26 features a wide range of events designed to challenge creativity, critical thinking, teamwork, communication, and technical skills.

Students can participate in cultural competitions, academic contests, artistic showcases, technology-based challenges, and interactive team events.

Each event is designed to provide participants with opportunities to demonstrate their abilities while engaging in a competitive and supportive environment.

The event lineup aims to create memorable experiences while encouraging innovation, collaboration, and personal growth.
