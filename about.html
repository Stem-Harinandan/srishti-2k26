About Srishti 2K26

Srishti 2K26 is the flagship interschool fest hosted by Saraswathi Vidyalaya, bringing together students from different schools to celebrate talent, creativity, innovation, competition, and collaboration.

Over the years, Srishti has grown into a recognized platform where students can participate in a wide variety of academic, cultural, artistic, and skill-based events. The festival creates opportunities for young minds to showcase their abilities, challenge themselves, and interact with students from diverse backgrounds.

The name "Srishti" represents creation, imagination, and the spirit of transforming ideas into reality. Reflecting this vision, the festival encourages participants to explore new possibilities through performance, leadership, teamwork, innovation, communication, and creative expression.

Saraswathi Vidyalaya has established itself as one of the prominent educational institutions in Thiruvananthapuram, with thousands of students and a strong emphasis on both academic and extracurricular excellence. The school has consistently hosted events, exhibitions, competitions, cultural programs, and student-led initiatives that encourage holistic development.

Srishti continues this tradition by providing a stage where students can compete, learn, collaborate, and build meaningful experiences beyond the classroom. The festival aims to inspire confidence, curiosity, creativity, and excellence while creating lasting memories for participants and visitors alike.

Srishti 2K26 celebrates the energy, imagination, and potential of students, bringing together competition, culture, innovation, and community in one shared experience.
